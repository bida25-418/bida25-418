About LexxPizza

LexxPizza was started in 2023 by a young pizza lover right here in Gaborone, Botswana. What began as a small kitchen experiment has grown into a neighborhood favorite.

Our mission is simple: serve delicious, affordable pizzas made with fresh local ingredients. We believe everyone deserves a good pizza without spending too much.

From our classic Margherita to our loaded Meat Lovers, every pizza is made with care and love. Thank you for supporting local! 🇧🇼
