About LexxPizza

LexxPizza was founded in 2022 by a young pizza lover right here in Gaborone, Botswana. What began as a small kitchen experiment has grown into a neighborhood favorite.

Our mission is simple: serve delicious, affordable pizzas made with fresh local ingredients. We believe everyone deserves a good pizza without spending too much.
